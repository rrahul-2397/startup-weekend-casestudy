What actually changed

2023 — sprint version

  • Coral used for text, buttons, headers and states alike — no semantic meaning, 3.9:1 contrast on white
  • Empty state says "Nothing to show!!" — a dead end
  • Nagging interval hidden as a stepper button in the profile
  • Personality lives only in one lock-screen mockup
  • Value-prop screen with stock illustration
  • Task rows: title + time, no state, no hierarchy between overdue and upcoming

2026 — revisit

  • Coral = "Frank wants something": actions, lateness, nags. Text is warm ink at 12:1
  • Zero state celebrates and sells the streak — every state has a job
  • Nag level is a first-class setting; each option previews Frank's actual voice
  • Personality is systemic: greeting, counters, previews, buttons ("Start nagging me")
  • Splash states the promise in eight words — no illustration needed
  • Late tasks escalate visually: tinted card, nag counter, "You're late" badge
